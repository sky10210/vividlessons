Access model

How teacher licensing works.

This explains the system clearly before purchase so teachers understand what they receive.

Step 1

Teacher buys a module or bundle

Shopify checkout handles payment. Each product page should describe the module, estimated time, included files, and grade/course fit.

Step 2

Teacher receives login credentials

The teacher gets a unique username and password for the purchased module or bundle.

Step 3

One-year license starts on first use

The first successful login activates access for one year. After login, the teacher sees launch buttons, QR codes, student links, and teacher resources.
