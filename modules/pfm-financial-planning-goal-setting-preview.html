Sample Lesson: What Is a Financial Goal?

A financial goal is a money-related outcome someone plans for. It could involve saving, spending, earning, borrowing, investing, or reducing debt.

Key idea: A goal becomes stronger when it includes a clear target, a reason, and a timeline.

Sample Question 1

Sample Question 2

Sample Question 3
